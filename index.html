Liu-Di LU

Postdoctoral Fellow in Applied Mathematics

Section of Mathematics, University of Geneva

I am currently a postdoctoral fellow in University of Geneva, Section of Mathematics under the supervision of Martin Jakob Gander. I am interested in studying the convergence of Domain Decomposition Methods and their applications on PDE-constrainted optimization problems. Meanwhile, I am also working on space-time methods for the numerical solution of PDE problems.

In September 2021, I received my Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics at Laboratory Jacques-Louis Lions under the supervision of Julien Salomon and Olivier Bernard. I was also member of the INRIA Paris Team ANGE and INRIA Sophia Antipolis Team BIOCORE. I focused on the modelling, optimization and control problems issue from industrial microalgae production.
